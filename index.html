Star Wars: BB-8 Adventures
  • You are playing as BB-8, Star Wars: The Force Awakens droid, who needs to steal Secret Empire Plans
  • You need to score 5 points at level 1
  • Level 2 is survival! Earn as much points, as you can
  • All the appropriate textures, sounds, music and fictional characters belong to LucasArts and Disney Corp. All of it was used "just for fun" in a profitless manner
  • Stormtrooper and (spoiler alert! :) level 2 boss textures were taken from cubeecraft.com. Thank you guys for your great site!
